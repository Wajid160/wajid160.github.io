    About Me

    I'm focused on pushing the boundaries of what AI can achieve. My recent work centers on agentic AI—developing systems that operate autonomously and make independent decisions—alongside advanced generative AI techniques and multi-agent systems. I thrive on solving complex challenges and transforming innovative ideas into practical, real-world solutions. Driven by curiosity and a commitment to progress, I constantly seek new challenges that inspire creative thinking and technical excellence.

    In addition, I have built prototypes of Customer Support Task Scheduling and Data Retrieval Agents using publicly available data. These projects replicate key functionalities found in operational business systems, demonstrating my ability to reverse-engineer real-world scenarios and develop solutions that mirror industry standards.

    Together, my work reflects a blend of technical rigor, innovation, and an unwavering dedication to advancing AI technology.
